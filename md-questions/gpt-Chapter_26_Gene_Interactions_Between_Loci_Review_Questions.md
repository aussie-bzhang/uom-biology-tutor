**Chapter 26 Review Questions
Genetic Analysis Involving Interactions Between Loci Affecting the Same Trait**

*First-Year Biology | Topic 3: Genes and Heredity | University of Melbourne lecture-based revision*

Learning focus: predicting outcomes of genetic crosses where multiple genes contribute to the same phenotype; distinguishing additive interactions, modifiers and epistasis; interpreting modified dihybrid ratios such as 9:3:3:1, 9:4:3 and 9:3:4.

# Practice Set 1 - Core Concepts: Multiple Genes and Modified Ratios
