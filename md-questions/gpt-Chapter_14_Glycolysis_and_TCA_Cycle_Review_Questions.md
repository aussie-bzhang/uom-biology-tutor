**Chapter 14 Review Questions
An Introduction to Glycolysis and the TCA Cycle**

*Based on Lecture 14: An introduction to glycolysis and the TCA cycle*

**Study focus.** This chapter introduces cellular respiration through glycolysis, pyruvate oxidation, and the citric acid cycle/TCA cycle/Krebs cycle. Focus on where glycolysis occurs, its two phases, ATP and NADH production, regulation by phosphofructokinase, the role of acetyl-CoA, TCA cycle outputs, and how these pathways connect catabolism with anabolism.
