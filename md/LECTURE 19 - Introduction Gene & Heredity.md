Learning outcomes
By the end of this lecture, you will be able to recognise that genetics involves
studying multiple aspects of genes, including their DNA sequence, expression,
transmission, function and interactions, and how these combine to influence
traits
This includes being able to:
• Recall that genes are sequences of DNA that are expressed as RNA and
polypeptide products
• Recognise that genes are carried on chromosomes and transmitted from one
generation to another
• Recognise that genetic crosses are useful to examine how different genes and their
variants can contribute to traits
