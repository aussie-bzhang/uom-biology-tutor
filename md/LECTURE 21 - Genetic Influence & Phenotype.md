Learning outcomes
By the end of this lecture, you will be able to explain the relationship between genes,
chromosomes, alleles, ploidy and phenotypes.
This includes being able to:
• Distinguish the key genetic terms used to describe genotypes and phenotypes.
• Describe the concept of dominance and recessivity, as well as incomplete and co-
dominance at the molecular level.
