Learning outcomes
By the end of this lecture, you will be able to describe multicellular
organisms as a hierarchy of specialised cell types comprising distinct
tissues and coordinating to form functional organs.
This includes being able to:
• Compare and contrast the four different tissues found in animals and
explain how different tissue types combine to form a functional organ.
• Compare and contrast the three tissue types found in flowering plants
and describe how these are integrated into a functioning leaf.
