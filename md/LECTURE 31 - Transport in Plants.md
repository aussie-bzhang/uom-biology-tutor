Learning outcomes
By the end of this lecture, you will be able to explain how the transport
needs of plants are met by the vascular tissue.
This includes being able to:
• Describe the transport needs of plants.
• Explain the origins, benefits and consequences of vascular tissue
including water and nutrient transport systems.
• Compare and contrast active and passive transport processes in
plants and the connections between them.
