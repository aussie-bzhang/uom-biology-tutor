Summary
• Photosynthesis is the ultimate source of all
chemical energy and organic molecules
available to photoautotrophs, such as
plants, and to virtually all other organisms
as well.
• It also constantly replenishes the supply of
oxygen in the atmosphere, vital to all
aerobic organisms.
• Photosynthesis occurs in 2 stages:
• The light dependent reactions and the
carbon fixation reactions.
• Plants have evolved to suit different
climates resulting in different pathways.
