Learning outcomes
By the end of this lecture, you will be able to interpret information about mutations
and predict their likely consequences on the function of genes.
This includes being able to:
• Recognise the importance of mutations as the primary source of genetic variation
• Describe the different terms used to classify mutations
• Explain how DNA mutations affect RNA and polypeptide sequence
