# Animal Tissues

## Overview
Animal tissues are categorized into four types: epithelial, connective, muscle, and nervous. Each type has distinct structures and functions, contributing to the overall organization and function of the organism.

## Key points
- Epithelial tissues cover and line surfaces.
- Connective tissues provide support and protection.
- Muscle tissues enable movement.
- Nervous tissues transmit signals.
