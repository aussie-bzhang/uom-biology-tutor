# Prokaryotic Cell

## Overview
Prokaryotic cells are characterized by a cell membrane, DNA located in the nucleoid, cytoplasm, and ribosomes. They lack membrane-bound organelles and have various structures like cell walls, capsules, flagella, and pili.

## Key points
- Prokaryotic cells have a cell membrane
- DNA is located in the nucleoid
- Cytoplasm contains the rest of the cell contents
- Ribosomes are sites of protein synthesis
- Prokaryotic cells lack membrane-bound organelles
- Cell walls, capsules, flagella, and pili are common structures
