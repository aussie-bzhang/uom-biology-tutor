# Lysosomes

## Overview
Lysosomes are single-membrane-bound organelles that contain hydrolytic enzymes. They break down materials ingested by endocytosis or recycle old organelles through autophagy. Lysosomes have an acidic interior and are involved in digestion and recycling within the cell.

## Key points
- Lysosomes are single-membrane-bound organelles.
- Lysosomes contain hydrolytic enzymes.
- Lysosomes break down materials and recycle old organelles.
