# Physiology of Circulatory Systems

## Overview
Circulatory systems consist of a pump, circulatory fluid, exchange surfaces, tubular vessels, mechanisms to return fluid to the pump, and elements to structure flow and direct fluid. These components work together to ensure efficient transport of substances throughout the body.

## Key points
- Circulatory systems have a pump, fluid, exchange surfaces, vessels, mechanisms to return fluid, and elements to structure flow.
- The components of the circulatory system are essential for the transport of substances in animals.
- The circulatory system ensures the delivery of nutrients, oxygen, and removal of waste products.
