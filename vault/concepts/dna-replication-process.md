# DNA Replication Process

## Overview
DNA replication is the process by which a cell duplicates its genetic material. It is semi-conservative, meaning each new DNA molecule consists of one strand from the original molecule and one newly synthesized strand. Nucleotides are added in a 5’ to 3’ direction by DNA polymerase III.

## Key points
- DNA replication is a semi-conservative process
- Nucleotides are added in a 5’ to 3’ direction
- DNA polymerase III synthesizes DNA in one direction
