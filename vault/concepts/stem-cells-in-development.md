# Role of Stem Cells in Animal Development

## Overview
Stem cells are undifferentiated cells that can divide indefinitely and differentiate into various cell types. In animal development, stem cells are crucial for the formation of tissues and organs. They also play a role in the maintenance and repair of tissues throughout the life of an organism.

## Key points
- Stem cells are undifferentiated cells that can differentiate into various cell types.
- Stem cells are essential for the formation of tissues and organs.
- Stem cells contribute to the maintenance and repair of tissues.
