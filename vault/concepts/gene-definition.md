# Definition of a Gene

## Overview
A gene is a specific sequence of DNA that contains the instructions for making RNA and protein, which are essential for the function and traits of an organism. Genes are the fundamental units of heredity and are located on chromosomes.

## Key points
- Genes are sequences of DNA that encode RNA and protein.
- Genes are the units of heredity and are found on chromosomes.
- Genetic information is transcribed into RNA and translated into proteins.
