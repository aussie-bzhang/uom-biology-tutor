# Feedback Inhibition

## Overview
Feedback inhibition is a regulatory mechanism in which the end product of a metabolic pathway inhibits an enzyme early in the pathway, thereby controlling the overall flow of metabolism. This mechanism helps maintain homeostasis by preventing the overproduction of certain metabolites.

## Key points
- Feedback inhibition occurs when the end product of a pathway inhibits an enzyme early in the pathway
- Feedback inhibition is a form of negative regulation that controls the flow of metabolism
- Feedback inhibition helps maintain homeostasis by preventing overproduction of metabolites
