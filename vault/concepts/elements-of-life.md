# Elements of Life

## Overview
The fundamental building blocks of life are carbon, hydrogen, oxygen, nitrogen, phosphorus, and sulfur. These elements are essential for the formation of biological molecules and are present in all known life forms.

## Key points
- Carbon, hydrogen, oxygen, nitrogen, phosphorus, and sulfur are the primary elements of life
- These elements are the foundation for the formation of macromolecules
