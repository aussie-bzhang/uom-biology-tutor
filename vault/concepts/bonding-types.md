# Bonding Types in Biochemistry

## Overview
Chemical bonds are the forces that hold atoms together in molecules. Bond types include ionic, polar covalent, and non-polar covalent, which are differentiated by the electronegativity difference between atoms.

## Key points
- Ionic bonds result from the transfer of electrons, polar covalent bonds involve unequal sharing of electrons, and non-polar covalent bonds involve equal sharing of electrons
- Electronegativity differences determine the type of bond formed between atoms
