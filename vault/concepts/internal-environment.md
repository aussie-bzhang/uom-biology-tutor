# Internal Aqueous Environment

## Overview
The internal environment of multicellular organisms serves as a medium for gas exchange and nutrient distribution. It is maintained by homeostasis, ensuring that the environment remains stable and conducive to cellular function.

## Key points
- Internal environment facilitates gas exchange and nutrient distribution
- Homeostasis maintains stability of the internal environment
