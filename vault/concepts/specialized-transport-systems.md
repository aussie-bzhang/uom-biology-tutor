# Specialized Transport Systems

## Overview
Specialized transport systems, such as circulatory systems in animals and phloem in plants, play a crucial role in ensuring that substances from specialized tissues and organs reach cells throughout the organism. These systems maintain high concentration gradients for diffusion and facilitate efficient gas exchange and nutrient distribution.

## Key points
- Transport systems deliver substances to cells
- Maintain concentration gradients for diffusion
- Facilitate gas exchange and nutrient distribution
