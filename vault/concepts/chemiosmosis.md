# Chemiosmosis

## Overview
Chemiosmosis is the process by which ATP is synthesized using the energy from the proton gradient generated by the electron transport chain. This mechanism couples the flow of electrons with the synthesis of ATP, providing the energy needed for cellular processes.

## Key points
- Chemiosmosis occurs in the mitochondrial inner membrane
- It uses the proton gradient to drive ATP synthesis
- ATP synthase is the enzyme responsible for ATP synthesis
- The proton gradient is created by the electron transport chain
