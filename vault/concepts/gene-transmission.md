# Gene Transmission

## Overview
Genetic information is transmitted from parents to offspring through the process of meiosis, which produces gametes with half the number of chromosomes. This ensures that each offspring inherits a unique combination of genetic material from both parents.

## Key points
- Genetic information is passed from parents to offspring.
- Meiosis is the process that produces gametes with half the number of chromosomes.
- Offspring inherit a combination of genetic material from both parents.
