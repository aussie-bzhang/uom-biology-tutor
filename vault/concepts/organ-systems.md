# Organ Systems

## Overview
Organ systems are composed of multiple organs that work together to perform specific functions. Examples include the respiratory, circulatory, and digestive systems, each essential for the overall health and survival of the organism.

## Key points
- Organ systems are groups of organs.
- Systems work together to perform complex functions.
