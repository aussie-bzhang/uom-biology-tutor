# Transcription

## Overview
Transcription is the process by which DNA is copied into mRNA. This process is essential for the expression of genes and the production of proteins.

## Key points
- Transcription involves the synthesis of mRNA from a DNA template.
- The enzyme RNA polymerase catalyzes the formation of mRNA.
- Transcription is a critical step in gene expression.
