# Diversity of Multicellular Organisms

## Overview
Multicellular organisms exhibit a wide range of diversity, from simple colonial organisms to complex animals and plants. This diversity reflects the various evolutionary paths and adaptations that have occurred over time.

## Key points
- Multicellular organisms display a wide range of diversity.
- Diversity reflects different evolutionary paths and adaptations.
- Examples include colonial organisms, animals, and plants.
