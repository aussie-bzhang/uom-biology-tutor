# Types of Cell Surface Receptors

## Overview
Cell surface receptors are proteins that bind to specific ligands and initiate signal transduction. They can be categorized into ion channel receptors, enzyme-coupled receptors, and G protein-coupled receptors, each with unique mechanisms for signal transduction.

## Key points
- Ion channel receptors allow the passage of ions across the cell membrane in response to ligand binding.
- Enzyme-coupled receptors activate enzymes upon ligand binding, leading to a cascade of intracellular events.
- G protein-coupled receptors activate G proteins, which in turn modulate intracellular signaling pathways.
