# Redox Reactions in Bioenergetics

## Overview
Redox reactions, involving the transfer of electrons, are crucial in bioenergetics. They are involved in the electron transport chain and the production of ATP, providing the energy needed for cellular processes.

## Key points
- Redox reactions involve the transfer of electrons
- Redox reactions are essential for ATP production and cellular energy
