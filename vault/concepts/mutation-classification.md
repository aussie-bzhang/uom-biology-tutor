# Classification of Mutations

## Overview
Mutations can be classified based on how they occur (spontaneous or induced), whether they can be passed to offspring, their size, and their location within genes. This classification helps in understanding the nature and impact of mutations.

## Key points
- Mutations are classified by their occurrence, heritability, size, and location
- Classification helps in understanding the nature and impact of mutations
