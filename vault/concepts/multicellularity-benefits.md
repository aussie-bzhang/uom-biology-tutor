# Benefits of Multicellularity

## Overview
The benefits of multicellularity include increased size, functional specialization, and the ability to perform complex tasks that single cells cannot. This leads to more efficient energy use and the potential for more complex organisms.

## Key points
- Multicellularity allows for increased size and complexity.
- Functional specialization is a key benefit of multicellularity.
- Multicellularity enhances efficiency and complexity.
