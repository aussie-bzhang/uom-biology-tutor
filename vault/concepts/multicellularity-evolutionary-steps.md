# Evolutionary Steps to Multicellularity

## Overview
The transition to multicellularity likely involved several steps, including cell aggregation, intercellular communication, and cell specialization. These steps led to the loss of independent cell viability and the emergence of complex multicellular organisms.

## Key points
- The transition to multicellularity involved aggregation, communication, and specialization.
- These steps led to the loss of independent cell viability.
- The result was the emergence of complex multicellular organisms.
