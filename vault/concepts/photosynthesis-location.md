# Location of Photosynthesis

## Overview
Photosynthesis occurs in the chloroplasts of plant cells, algae, and cyanobacteria. Chloroplasts are primarily found in the mesophyll cells of leaves, where they contain the pigment chlorophyll, which captures light energy.

## Key points
- Photosynthesis takes place in chloroplasts
- Chloroplasts are abundant in mesophyll cells of leaves
- Chlorophyll is the primary pigment involved in capturing light energy
