# Evolution of Life

## Overview
The evolution of life on Earth is a process that began with the origin of life about 4 billion years ago, with prokaryotes appearing around 3.5 billion years ago, and eukaryotic life arising 2.1 billion years ago. This evolution is driven by natural selection and results in the diversity of life we see today.

## Key points
- Life on Earth arose soon after the appearance of liquid water
- Prokaryotes appeared around 3.5 billion years ago
- Eukaryotic life arose 2.1 billion years ago
- Evolution occurs by natural selection
- Life has evolved from primitive forms over 4 billion years
