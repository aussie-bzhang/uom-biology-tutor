# Developmental Mechanisms in Other Contexts

## Overview
Developmental mechanisms are not limited to early development but are also involved in tissue maintenance, cancer, tissue regeneration, and tissue engineering. These processes provide insights into the regulation of cell proliferation, differentiation, and tissue formation, and have applications in regenerative medicine and tissue engineering.

## Key points
- Developmental mechanisms are involved in tissue maintenance, cancer, tissue regeneration, and tissue engineering.
- These processes provide insights into cell regulation and tissue formation.
- Developmental mechanisms have applications in regenerative medicine and tissue engineering.
