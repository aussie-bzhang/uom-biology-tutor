# Gene Modifiers

## Overview
Modifier genes can influence the expression of other genes, affecting traits such as pigment distribution. In the context of coat color, modifier genes can determine the type and distribution of pigment granules.

## Key points
- Modifier genes can influence the expression of other genes.
- Modifier genes affect traits like pigment distribution.
