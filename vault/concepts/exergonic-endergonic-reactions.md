# Exergonic and Endergonic Reactions

## Overview
Exergonic reactions release energy and are spontaneous, while endergonic reactions absorb energy and are nonspontaneous. These reactions are fundamental to metabolism, with exergonic reactions providing the energy needed for endergonic reactions.

## Key points
- Exergonic reactions release energy and are spontaneous
- Endergonic reactions absorb energy and are nonspontaneous
- Exergonic reactions drive endergonic reactions in metabolism
