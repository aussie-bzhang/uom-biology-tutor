# Chloroplast Evolution

## Overview
Chloroplasts are double-membrane-bound organelles responsible for photosynthesis. They likely originated from the primary endosymbiosis of a photosynthetic cyanobacteria. Some chloroplasts also evolved through secondary endosymbiosis, where a eukaryotic cell engulfed another eukaryotic cell with a chloroplast.

## Key points
- Chloroplasts are double-membrane-bound structures
- They are the site of photosynthesis
- Chloroplasts evolved from primary endosymbiosis
- Some chloroplasts evolved through secondary endosymbiosis
