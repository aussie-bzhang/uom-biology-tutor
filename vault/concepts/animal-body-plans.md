# Animal Body Plans in Development

## Overview
Animal body plans refer to the general structure of an organism, including the arrangement of organ systems and the integrated functioning of its parts. Body plans are categorized based on symmetry (radial or bilateral), body cavity structure (coelomate or acoelomate), segmentation, type of appendages, and type of nervous system.

## Key points
- Animal body plans refer to the general structure of an organism.
- Body plans are categorized based on symmetry, body cavity structure, segmentation, appendages, and nervous system.
