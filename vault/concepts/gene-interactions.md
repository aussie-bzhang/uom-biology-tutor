# Gene Interactions

## Overview
Different genes can interact through biochemical pathways, developmental pathways, and signaling pathways to influence the same biological characteristic. These interactions can lead to complex traits that are not easily predicted by simple genetic models.

## Key points
- Genes can interact to influence the same trait.
- Biochemical, developmental, and signaling pathways mediate gene interactions.
- Complex traits can result from the interactions of multiple genes.
