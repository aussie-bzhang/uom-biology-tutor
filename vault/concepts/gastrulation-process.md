# Gastrulation Process in Animal Development

## Overview
Gastrulation is the process by which a blastula transforms into a gastrula, involving the movement of cells into a multi-layered embryo with three germ layers. This process is critical for establishing the body axes and the three primary cell types that will give rise to different tissues and organs.

## Key points
- Gastrulation transforms a blastula into a gastrula.
- Gastrulation establishes the three germ layers: ectoderm, mesoderm, and endoderm.
- Gastrulation is essential for establishing body axes and cell differentiation.
