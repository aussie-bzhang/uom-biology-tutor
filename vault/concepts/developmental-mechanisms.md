# Developmental Mechanisms in Animal Development

## Overview
The four major events of development are determination, differentiation, morphogenesis, and growth. Determination is the process of specifying cell fate, differentiation is the process of cells becoming specialized, morphogenesis is the process of shaping the body, and growth is the increase in size of the organism.

## Key points
- Determination specifies cell fate.
- Differentiation leads to cell specialization.
- Morphogenesis shapes the body.
- Growth increases the size of the organism.
