# Mitochondria Evolution

## Overview
Mitochondria are double-membrane-bound organelles responsible for cellular respiration. They likely originated from the primary endosymbiosis of a purple bacteria. Their semi-autonomous nature, DNA, and protein synthesis machinery support this theory.

## Key points
- Mitochondria are double-membrane-bound structures
- They are the site of cellular respiration
- Mitochondria evolved from primary endosymbiosis
- Mitochondria have their own DNA and protein synthesis machinery
