# Impact of Mutations on Gene Function

## Overview
Mutations can have various effects on gene function, ranging from no effect to loss-of-function, gain-of-function, or altered expression. The impact depends on the type of mutation and its location within the gene.

## Key points
- Mutations can have various effects on gene function
- The impact depends on the type and location of the mutation
