# Cleavage Process in Animal Development

## Overview
Cleavage is the rapid division of the zygote into smaller cells without growth. This process leads to the formation of a blastula, which is a hollow ball of cells with a fluid-filled cavity. Cleavage is essential for the early stages of development and the establishment of the body plan.

## Key points
- Cleavage is a series of rapid cell divisions without growth.
- Cleavage results in the formation of a blastula.
- Cleavage is crucial for establishing the body plan.
