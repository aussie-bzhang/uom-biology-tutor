# Cell Theory

## Overview
Cell theory is a foundational concept in biology that posits cells as the fundamental unit of life, all living organisms are composed of cells, all cells arise from pre-existing cells, and modern cells evolved from a common ancestor.

## Key points
- Cells are the fundamental unit of life
- All living organisms are comprised of cells
- All cells come from pre-existing cells
- Modern cells evolved from a common ancestor
