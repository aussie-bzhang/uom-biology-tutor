# Plant Embryogenesis and Morphogenesis

## Overview
Plant embryogenesis involves a series of steps that establish the basic body plan of the plant, including the formation of the apical-basal axis and the establishment of radial tissue patterns. Morphogenesis in plants is guided by cell communication and differential gene expression, leading to the development of specific tissues and organs.

## Key points
- Plant embryogenesis establishes the basic body plan along two principal axes: apical-basal and radial.
- Morphogenesis in plants involves cell division, elongation, and differentiation, guided by environmental cues and gene regulation.
- Cell communication plays a crucial role in plant morphogenesis.
