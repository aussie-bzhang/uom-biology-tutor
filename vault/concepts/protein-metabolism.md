# Protein Metabolism

## Overview
Protein metabolism involves the breakdown of proteins into amino acids and the synthesis of proteins from amino acids. This process is vital for maintaining cellular structure and function, and essential amino acids must be obtained from the diet.

## Key points
- Protein catabolism breaks down proteins into amino acids
- Protein synthesis involves the attachment of amino acids to a carbon skeleton
- Essential amino acids are required for protein synthesis and must be obtained from the diet
