# Endomembrane System

## Overview
The endomembrane system includes the nuclear envelope, endoplasmic reticulum, Golgi apparatus, lysosomes, and plant vacuoles. It is responsible for the synthesis, packaging, and distribution of proteins, lipids, and carbohydrates. Endocytosis is the process of packing materials into a vesicle for transport into a cell, while exocytosis is the release of materials and waste out of a cell.

## Key points
- The endomembrane system includes the nuclear envelope, ER, Golgi apparatus, lysosomes, and plant vacuoles.
- The endomembrane system synthesizes, packages, and distributes macromolecules.
- Endocytosis is the process of packing materials into a vesicle for transport into a cell.
- Exocytosis is the release of materials and waste out of a cell.
