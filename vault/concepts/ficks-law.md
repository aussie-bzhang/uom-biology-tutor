# Fick's Law

## Overview
Fick's law describes the rate of diffusion as a function of surface area, partial pressure gradient, diffusion coefficient, and diffusion distance. It provides a mathematical framework for understanding how gas exchange occurs across specialized exchange surfaces.

## Key points
- Rate of diffusion is influenced by surface area, partial pressure gradient, diffusion coefficient, and diffusion distance
- Fick's law explains the physical factors controlling gas exchange
