# Post-Transcriptional Regulation

## Overview
Post-transcriptional regulation occurs after transcription and involves processes such as mRNA processing, transport, and stability, which can affect the final amount of protein produced.

## Key points
- mRNA processing includes splicing, capping, and polyadenylation, which can alter the mRNA sequence and stability.
- Transport of mRNA from the nucleus to the cytoplasm is regulated to ensure proper protein synthesis.
- mRNA stability can be regulated by various factors, including RNA-binding proteins and microRNAs.
