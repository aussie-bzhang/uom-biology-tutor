# Enzyme Catalysis and Mechanism

## Overview
Enzyme catalysis involves the binding of a substrate to the active site of an enzyme, which lowers the activation energy required for the reaction. The lock-and-key and induced-fit models describe how enzymes interact with substrates, with the induced-fit model emphasizing the dynamic nature of the enzyme-substrate complex.

## Key points
- Enzymes lower the activation energy of reactions.
- The lock-and-key model suggests a rigid enzyme-substrate interaction.
- The induced-fit model describes a dynamic enzyme-substrate complex.
