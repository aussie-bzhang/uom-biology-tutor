# Genetic Terms and Concepts

## Overview
Genetic terms such as genotypes, phenotypes, alleles, and ploidy are fundamental to understanding how traits are inherited. Genotypes refer to the genetic makeup of an organism, while phenotypes are the observable traits. Alleles are different versions of a gene, and ploidy describes the number of sets of chromosomes in a cell. These terms are crucial for interpreting genetic information and predicting inheritance patterns.

## Key points
- Genotypes are the genetic makeup of an organism, while phenotypes are the observable traits.
- Alleles are different versions of a gene, and ploidy describes the number of sets of chromosomes.
- Understanding these terms is essential for interpreting genetic information and predicting inheritance patterns.
