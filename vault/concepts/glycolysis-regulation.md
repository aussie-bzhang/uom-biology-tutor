# Regulation of Glycolysis

## Overview
Glycolysis is regulated by allosteric enzymes at key points, ensuring that the process is controlled and efficient. This regulation is essential for maintaining cellular energy homeostasis and responding to metabolic demands.

## Key points
- Glycolysis is regulated by allosteric enzymes.
- Control points in glycolysis help maintain energy balance.
- Regulation is crucial for cellular metabolism.
