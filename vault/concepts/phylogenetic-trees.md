# Phylogenetic Trees

## Overview
Phylogenetic trees are diagrams that represent the evolutionary relationships between organisms. They show the divergence of life over time and the common ancestry of different species.

## Key points
- Phylogenetic trees represent evolutionary relationships
- Trees show divergence of life over time
- Trees indicate common ancestry
- Fungi are more closely related to plants than to animals
