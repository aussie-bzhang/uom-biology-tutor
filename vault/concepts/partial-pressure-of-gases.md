# Partial Pressure of Gases

## Overview
Partial pressure is the pressure exerted by a single gas in a mixture of gases. In gas exchange, partial pressure gradients between gases drive the diffusion process. Understanding partial pressure is crucial for explaining how gases move across specialized exchange surfaces.

## Key points
- Partial pressure is the pressure exerted by a single gas in a mixture
- Partial pressure gradients drive diffusion in gas exchange
