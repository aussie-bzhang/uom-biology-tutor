# Carbohydrate Metabolism

## Overview
Carbohydrate metabolism involves the breakdown of glucose through glycolysis to produce energy and the synthesis of glucose through gluconeogenesis. These processes are critical for providing energy to cells and for the synthesis of carbon skeletons for other molecules.

## Key points
- Glycolysis breaks down glucose into pyruvate, releasing energy
- Gluconeogenesis synthesizes glucose from non-carbohydrate sources
- Carbohydrates are a primary source of energy for cellular processes
