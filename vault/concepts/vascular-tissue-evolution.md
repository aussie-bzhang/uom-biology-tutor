# Evolution of Vascular Tissue

## Overview
Vascular tissue evolved to meet the transport needs of plants. It provides rigid structural support and efficient transport of water and nutrients. The evolution of vascular tissue allowed plants to grow taller and compete more effectively in diverse environments.

## Key points
- Vascular tissue evolved to support the growth of tall plants.
- It provides structural support and efficient transport of water and nutrients.
- The evolution of vascular tissue was a key factor in the success of land plants.
