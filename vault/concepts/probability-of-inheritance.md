# Probability of Inheritance

## Overview
The probability of inheritance is calculated based on the genetic makeup of individuals and their family history. It involves understanding the likelihood of a trait appearing in offspring based on the inheritance patterns observed in pedigrees.

## Key points
- Probability of inheritance is determined by the combination of alleles from both parents.
- Understanding the probability of inheritance is crucial for genetic counseling and making informed reproductive decisions.
- Probability calculations can be complex and require knowledge of Mendelian genetics and pedigrees.
