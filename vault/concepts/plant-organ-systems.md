# Plant Organ Systems

## Overview
Plant organ systems, such as the root system and shoot system, are composed of organs that work together to meet the plant's metabolic and structural needs, including nutrient absorption, anchoring, and growth.

## Key points
- Plant organ systems are composed of organs.
- Systems support plant growth and function.
