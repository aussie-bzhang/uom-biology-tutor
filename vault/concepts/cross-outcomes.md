# Predicting Genetic Cross Outcomes

## Overview
The outcomes of genetic crosses can be predicted using genetic maps. By analyzing the map distances between genes, one can estimate the likelihood of recombination events and the resulting genotypes of the offspring.

## Key points
- Genetic maps help predict the outcomes of genetic crosses.
- Recombination frequency is used to estimate the likelihood of recombination events.
- Predicting cross outcomes is essential for understanding genetic inheritance.
