# Intermolecular Forces

## Overview
Intermolecular forces are the attractions between molecules. They include dipole-dipole forces, London dispersion forces, and hydrogen bonding, which play crucial roles in the structure and function of biological molecules.

## Key points
- Dipole-dipole forces occur between polar molecules, London dispersion forces are present in non-polar molecules, and hydrogen bonding is a special type of dipole-dipole interaction involving hydrogen
