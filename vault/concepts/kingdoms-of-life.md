# Kingdoms of Life

## Overview
Eukarya is further classified into four kingdoms: Plantae, Protista, Animalia, and Fungi. Each kingdom has distinct characteristics such as cell type, nutrition, and cell wall composition.

## Key points
- Eukarya is further classified into four kingdoms
- Plantae are multicellular autotrophs with cell walls
- Protista are unicellular and can be autotrophs or heterotrophs
- Animalia are multicellular heterotrophs without cell walls
- Fungi are multicellular heterotrophs with cell walls
