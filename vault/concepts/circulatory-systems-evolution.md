# Evolution of Animal Circulatory Systems

## Overview
The animal vascular system has evolved from simple to complex structures, reflecting the increasing metabolic demands of animals. This evolution includes the development of closed circulatory systems and the evolution of chambered hearts.

## Key points
- The animal vascular system has evolved to meet the increasing metabolic demands of animals.
- Closed circulatory systems and chambered hearts are adaptations that enhance transport efficiency.
- The evolution of the vascular system is a response to the need for more efficient transport and energy usage.
