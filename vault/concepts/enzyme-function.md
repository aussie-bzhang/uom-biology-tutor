# Role of Enzymes in Cellular Processes

## Overview
Enzymes are essential for metabolism and digestion, acting as biological catalysts that speed up chemical reactions in cells. They control these reactions by lowering the activation energy required for the reactions to proceed, allowing for complex metabolic pathways to be regulated in a precise and efficient manner.

## Key points
- Enzymes are critical for metabolism and digestion.
- Enzymes not only speed up reactions but also control them.
- Enzymes enable complex metabolism through precise regulation.
