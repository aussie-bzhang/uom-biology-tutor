# Definition of Life

## Overview
Life is defined by a set of characteristics including the composition of common elements, cellular structure, genetic information, growth, responsiveness, molecular usage, energy extraction, population existence, and evolutionary capability.

## Key points
- Life is composed of a common set of elements
- Life is cellular
- Life contains genetic information
- Life grows and changes
- Life responds to the environment
- Life uses molecules and makes new molecules
- Life extracts energy and uses it
- Life exists in populations and can evolve
