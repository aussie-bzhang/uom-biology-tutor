# Origin of Life

## Overview
The origin of life is a topic of ongoing research, with theories including the spontaneous formation of life on Earth, facilitated by organic molecule formation, and the possibility of life originating from extraterrestrial sources such as comets or meteorites.

## Key points
- Life formed spontaneously on Earth
- Life may have originated from extraterrestrial sources
- The Miller-Urey experiment suggested life could form from organic molecules
- Comets and meteorites could have brought life to Earth
