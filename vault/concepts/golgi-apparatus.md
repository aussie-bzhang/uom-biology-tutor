# Golgi Apparatus

## Overview
The Golgi apparatus consists of flattened stacks of membrane called cisternae. It collects, packages, and distributes molecules that are synthesized in the ER. Vesicles arrive at the cis face and leave at the trans face, indicating the direction of packaging and distribution.

## Key points
- The Golgi apparatus consists of flattened stacks of membrane called cisternae.
- The Golgi apparatus packages and distributes molecules.
- Vesicles arrive at the cis face and leave at the trans face.
