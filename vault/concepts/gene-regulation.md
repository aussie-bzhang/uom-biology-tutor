# Control of Gene Expression

## Overview
Gene expression can be regulated at various levels, including transcription, RNA processing, and translation. This regulation is crucial for controlling cellular processes and ensuring proper development.

## Key points
- Gene expression is regulated to control protein production.
- Regulation occurs at multiple stages of gene expression.
- Gene regulation is important for cellular processes and development.
