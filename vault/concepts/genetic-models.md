# Genetic Models of Inheritance

## Overview
Genetic crosses involve mating individuals with different genetic variants to study inheritance patterns. Mendelian inheritance principles, such as dominance and segregation, are used to predict the likelihood of certain traits appearing in offspring.

## Key points
- Genetic crosses are used to study inheritance patterns.
- Mendelian inheritance principles explain how traits are passed from parents to offspring.
- Dominance and segregation are key concepts in Mendelian inheritance.
