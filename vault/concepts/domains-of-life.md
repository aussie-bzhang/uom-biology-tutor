# Domains of Life

## Overview
Life is categorized into three domains: Bacteria, Archaea, and Eukarya. Bacteria and Archaea are prokaryotic, lacking a nucleus and membrane-bound organelles, while Eukarya are eukaryotic, containing a nucleus and membrane-bound organelles.

## Key points
- Life is categorized into three domains: Bacteria, Archaea, and Eukarya
- Bacteria and Archaea are prokaryotic
- Eukarya are eukaryotic
- Archaea are more closely related to Eukarya than to Bacteria
