# Physical Properties of Water

## Overview
Water's unique physical properties, such as high specific heat, cohesion, and adhesion, are due to its ability to form hydrogen bonds. These properties make water an essential component for life on Earth.

## Key points
- Water's high specific heat and boiling point are due to hydrogen bonding
- Water's cohesion and adhesion are important for its role in biological systems
