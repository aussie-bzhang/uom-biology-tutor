# Consequences of Multicellularity

## Overview
The consequences of multicellularity include the need for complex transport systems, increased metabolic requirements, and the need for coordination and communication among cells. These challenges are met through specialized systems and regulatory mechanisms.

## Key points
- Multicellularity necessitates complex transport systems.
- Increased metabolic requirements are a consequence of multicellularity.
- Coordination and communication are crucial for multicellular organisms.
