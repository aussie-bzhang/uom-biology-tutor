# Bioenergetics

## Overview
Bioenergetics is the study of energy flow and transformation within living organisms. It involves understanding the various forms of energy, such as potential and kinetic energy, and how they are converted and transferred. ATP, the energy currency of the cell, plays a central role in this process, mediating energy transfer for cellular activities.

## Key points
- Energy is the capacity to cause change and can be converted from one form to another
- ATP is the primary energy carrier in cells, facilitating energy transfer for endergonic reactions
- Energy transfer is fundamental to cellular processes and metabolism
