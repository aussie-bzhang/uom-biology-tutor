# Plant Transport Needs

## Overview
Plants require efficient transport systems to deliver water, nutrients, and sugars throughout their structures. Water is essential for photosynthesis, cooling, and structural integrity. Nutrients are needed for growth and development, while sugars are transported from photosynthetic tissues to non-photosynthetic tissues.

## Key points
- Plants need water for photosynthesis, cooling, and structural support.
- Nutrients are transported to support growth and development.
- Sugars are transported from leaves to roots and other non-photosynthetic tissues.
