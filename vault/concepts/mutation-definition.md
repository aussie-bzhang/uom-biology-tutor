# Definition of Mutation

## Overview
A mutation is a change in the sequence, structure, or composition of an organism's genome. It can affect genes and may or may not be transmitted to offspring. Mutations are the primary source of genetic variation.

## Key points
- Mutations are changes in the genome
- They can affect genes and are the source of genetic variation
