# Enzyme Inhibition

## Overview
Enzyme inhibitors can affect reaction rates by binding to enzymes and preventing the binding of substrates. There are different types of inhibitors, including competitive, noncompetitive, and irreversible inhibitors, each with distinct mechanisms and effects on enzyme activity.

## Key points
- Enzyme inhibitors bind to enzymes and prevent substrate binding.
- Competitive, noncompetitive, and irreversible inhibitors affect enzyme activity differently.
- Enzyme inhibitors play a role in metabolic regulation.
