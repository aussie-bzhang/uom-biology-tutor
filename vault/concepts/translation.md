# Translation

## Overview
Translation is the process by which the information in mRNA is used to synthesize a protein. This process involves the conversion of mRNA codons into amino acids.

## Key points
- Translation occurs in the ribosome and involves the assembly of amino acids into a polypeptide chain.
- tRNA molecules bring amino acids to the ribosome based on the codons in mRNA.
- Translation is the final step in gene expression and protein synthesis.
