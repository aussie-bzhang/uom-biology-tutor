# Regulation of Animal Development

## Overview
Gene expression is a key regulatory mechanism in animal development. It determines cell fate and tissue formation by controlling the production of specific proteins. Understanding gene expression is crucial for understanding the complex processes of development.

## Key points
- Gene expression is a key regulatory mechanism in animal development.
- Gene expression determines cell fate and tissue formation.
- Understanding gene expression is essential for understanding development.
