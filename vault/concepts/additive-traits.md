# Additive Traits

## Overview
Additive traits are characterized by the independent contributions of different genes to a phenotype, which combine to produce a new trait. This can be observed in traits like flower color, where dominant traits combine to produce a new phenotype.

## Key points
- Additive traits involve the independent contributions of different genes to a phenotype.
- The combination of dominant traits can produce a new phenotype.
