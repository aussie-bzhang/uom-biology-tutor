# Plant Vacuoles

## Overview
Plant vacuoles are single-membrane-bound organelles similar to lysosomes. They contain hydrolytic enzymes and serve as degradative compartments. Plant vacuoles also store nutrients, pigments, and maintain cell turgor pressure.

## Key points
- Plant vacuoles are single-membrane-bound organelles.
- Plant vacuoles contain hydrolytic enzymes.
- Plant vacuoles store nutrients, pigments, and maintain cell turgor pressure.
