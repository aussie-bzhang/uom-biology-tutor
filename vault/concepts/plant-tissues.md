# Plant Tissues

## Overview
Plant tissues are categorized into three types: dermal, vascular, and ground. These tissues are integrated into organs such as roots, stems, and leaves, each with specific functions essential for plant survival and growth.

## Key points
- Dermal tissues protect and exchange gases.
- Vascular tissues transport water and nutrients.
- Ground tissues support and store nutrients.
