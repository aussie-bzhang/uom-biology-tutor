# Types of Mutations

## Overview
Mutations can be categorized into different types based on their impact on the polypeptide sequence and gene function. Silent mutations have no effect, missense mutations change a single amino acid, nonsense mutations introduce a premature stop codon, and frameshift mutations alter the reading frame.

## Key points
- Silent mutations do not change the polypeptide sequence
- Missense mutations change a single amino acid
- Nonsense mutations introduce a premature stop codon
- Frameshift mutations alter the reading frame
