# Animal Transport Needs

## Overview
Multicellular animals require complex transport systems to overcome the limitations of diffusion due to their size and internal cell placement. These systems facilitate gas exchange, nutrient delivery, waste removal, and intercellular communication.

## Key points
- Multicellular organisms face challenges with diffusion due to their size and internal cell placement.
- Complex transport systems are necessary for efficient gas exchange, nutrient mobilization, waste removal, and intercellular communication in animals.
- Animals require a transport system because direct diffusion to cells is not sufficient.
