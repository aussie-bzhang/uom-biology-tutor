# Capillary Exchange

## Overview
Capillary exchange is driven by pressure differences and osmotic forces. The balance of hydrostatic pressures and osmotic pressure determines whether substances are filtered out of the blood or reabsorbed into the blood.

## Key points
- Capillary exchange is driven by pressure differences and osmotic forces.
- Hydrostatic pressure and osmotic pressure determine the movement of substances across the capillary walls.
- Filtration and reabsorption are the processes by which substances are exchanged between the blood and the interstitial fluid.
