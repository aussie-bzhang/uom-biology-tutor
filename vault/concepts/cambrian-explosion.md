# Cambrian Explosion

## Overview
The Cambrian Explosion, which occurred approximately 541 million years ago, was a period of rapid diversification of life, with the appearance of most major animal groups that exist today.

## Key points
- The Cambrian Explosion was a period of rapid life diversification
- Most major animal groups appeared during the Cambrian
- The Cambrian Explosion is marked by well-preserved fossils
