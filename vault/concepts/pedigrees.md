# Pedigrees

## Overview
Pedigrees are graphical representations of family histories that help in understanding the inheritance of genetic traits. They are constructed using specific conventions and symbols to depict the relationships between family members and the presence or absence of a trait.

## Key points
- Pedigrees use symbols to represent individuals, their sex, and the presence of a trait.
- Interpreting pedigrees can reveal patterns of inheritance, such as dominant or recessive traits, and the likelihood of a trait appearing in future generations.
- Pedigrees are a valuable tool for genetic counseling and understanding the genetics of a population.
