# Effects of Mutations on DNA, RNA, and Polypeptide Sequences

## Overview
Mutations can lead to changes in the DNA sequence, which in turn affect the RNA and polypeptide sequences. This can result in silent, missense, nonsense, or frameshift mutations, each with different effects on gene function.

## Key points
- Mutations affect DNA, RNA, and polypeptide sequences
- Different types of mutations have varying effects on gene function
