# Limitations of Multicellularity

## Overview
Multicellularity presents limitations such as the need for complex transport systems, increased metabolic requirements, and the need for coordination among cells. These limitations are overcome through specialized systems and regulatory mechanisms.

## Key points
- Multicellularity requires complex transport systems.
- Increased metabolic requirements are a limitation.
- Coordination and communication are essential for overcoming limitations.
