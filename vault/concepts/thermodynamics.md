# Thermodynamics in Biology

## Overview
Thermodynamics, the study of energy transformations, is fundamental to understanding biological systems. The first law of thermodynamics states the conservation of energy, while the second law describes the increase in entropy, or disorder, in the universe. These principles apply to biological systems, where energy is conserved and transformed, and entropy increases over time.

## Key points
- The first law of thermodynamics states that energy cannot be created or destroyed, only transformed
- The second law of thermodynamics indicates that entropy in the universe always increases
