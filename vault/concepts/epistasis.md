# Epistasis

## Overview
Epistasis is a gene interaction where the expression of one gene is dependent on the presence of another gene. This can lead to complex patterns of inheritance and can be observed in traits like coat color in dogs and cats.

## Key points
- Epistasis is a gene interaction where the expression of one gene depends on another gene.
- Epistasis can lead to complex patterns of inheritance.
