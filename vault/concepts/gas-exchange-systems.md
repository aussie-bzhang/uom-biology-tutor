# Gas Exchange Systems in Multicellular Organisms

## Overview
Multicellular organisms have developed three key innovations to overcome the limitations of gas exchange: maintaining an internal aqueous environment, specialized transport systems, and specialized exchange surfaces. These adaptations allow for efficient gas exchange and nutrient distribution throughout the organism.

## Key points
- Three innovations: internal environment, specialized transport systems, specialized exchange surfaces
- Internal environment provides a medium for exchange
- Specialized transport systems ensure substances reach cells
- Specialized exchange surfaces maximize gas exchange
