# Lipid Metabolism

## Overview
Lipid metabolism includes the breakdown of fatty acids through beta-oxidation and the breakdown of triglycerides through lipolysis. These processes are essential for energy production and the transport of lipids in the body.

## Key points
- Beta-oxidation breaks down fatty acids into acetyl CoA, releasing energy
- Lipolysis breaks down triglycerides into glycerol and fatty acids
- Lipids serve as a long-term energy storage and transport medium
