# Genetic Variation

## Overview
Genetic variation arises from mutations, which can alter DNA sequences and lead to differences in genetic information. This variation is the raw material for evolution and is essential for the survival and adaptation of populations.

## Key points
- Mutations are the primary source of genetic variation.
- Genetic variation is crucial for evolution and adaptation.
- Understanding the sources and implications of genetic variation is fundamental to evolutionary biology.
